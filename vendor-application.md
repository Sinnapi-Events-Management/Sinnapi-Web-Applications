The Vendor application process should collect the following information.

For each vendor application we
track the following i.e businessName, location, Brief Bio / About Your Business, images (an
array of url strings), primary image url, videos (an array of url strings), profile image url, social
media links(can be an array of objects), primary category / service, categories/services (an array
of services), business owner fullname, business owner fullname, business owner email,
business owner phone, business owner phone number, Base of Operation / City, National id
url(uploaded as png, jpg, pdf), years in operation (options includes less than 1year, 1-3years, 3
-5 years, 5-10years, 10+years), Business Registration Number(optional if business not
registered), Tax Identification Number (optional), Bank Name, Account Name, Account Number,
Branch, Business Website, Instagram Profile, TikTok Profile, LinkedIn Profile, facebook profile,
client referees (optional - can be an array of objects with the following properties i.e full name,
phone number, email, event worked on, event date ), Upload Document for proof of work (pdf
file url), Are you an Icandy Masterclass Alumni? (optional yes or No), How do you price your
services? (optional can be the following fixed packages, hourly rates, custom quotation per
event, mix of the above), Starting Price (UGX), Service regions (should be a checkbox select
one or multiple choices from the following Kampala, Central Uganda, Eastern Uganda, Western
Uganda, Northern Uganda, Nationwide, East Africa, International), Typical Lead Time Needed
i.e How far in advance do clients need to book you? (Can select one of the following same
week,1 -2 weeks, 2-4 weeks, 1-3 months, 3+ months), Terms & Confirmation (must accept our
terms and conditions each listed with a checkbox i.e I confirm that all information provided is
accurate and truthful, I agree to Sinnapi's Vendor Terms of Service, I agree to Sinnapi's Payment &
Escrow Policy, I understand that false information will result in immediate removal from the platform).

Note that the vendor can either be an individual or registered business
