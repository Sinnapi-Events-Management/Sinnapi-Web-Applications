Our Story
Sinnapi was birthed after we had been in the wedding industry for 7+ years as events planners. We got to experience first hand and also hear from our clients their greatest pain points which are; 1. The inconvenience of finding authentic events service providers from the gross pool on social media and the market place. 2. The time it takes for one to connect with these service providers, establish trust, do due diligence before they use their services. There was also a struggle amongst the service providers to gain visibility due to the immense competition across the industry. This is what birthed Sinnapi, to create a home where all these businesses are visible, and their previous works seen - to simplify it for the consumer to locate them and easily plan and execute their event.

Our Impact on the Events Industry
Even before we officially launched on March 15th 2026, we had been able to channel business to the vendors on the waitlist through referrals of over UGX 300m.
